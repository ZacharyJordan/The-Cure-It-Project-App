Throat Cancer Foundation

The Throat Cancer Foundation was founded in 2012 by Jamie Rae, a survivor of throat cancer. His group aims to reduce the effect throat cancer has not only on the people who have it but everyone else around them too.

His inspiration came from a want to teach and inform people of ways to donate and learn about throat cancer, an underreported cancer.

The group is excelled in knowing up-to-date information regarding throat cancer and try to serve as a support group along with funding research to help end throat cancer. The group also wants to end HPV infections and has pushed for global HPV vaccination opportunities for all people.

Its scientists and doctors are some of the leading experts in their respective fields, and you can find a complete list of the members of the Throat Cancer Foundation here.
