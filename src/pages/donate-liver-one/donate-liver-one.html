Foundation for Liver Research

The Foundation for Liver Research has been operating on the front lines of the fight against liver disease for over 3 decades now.

They have committed themselves completely to funding ground-breaking research programs as well as providing laboratory space for the scientists who make such research possible.

The research made possible by those who donate is extensive and well documented. At the time of writing, they are involved in projects that involve 8 different areas of liver disease.

It is through research such as this that a meaningful advance against Liver Cancer and diseases like it can be made. You can help them achieve this tremendous goal by clicking the button below to donate.
