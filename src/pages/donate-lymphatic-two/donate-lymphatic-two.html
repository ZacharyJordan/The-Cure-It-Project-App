Lymphoma Foundation of America

The Lymphoma Foundation of America is an organization that has remained selflessly dedicated to the eradication of Lymphatic Cancers since its inception in 1986. They approach this goal on a variety of fronts to ensure that all avenues of progress can be considered.

The Lymphoma Foundation of America is a foundation started in 1986, created by a group of people with Lymphoma, dedicated to the betterment of the quality of life and treatment of Lymphoma patients just like them all across the nation.

Their operations are extensive, with the scope of their services including: one on one counseling, educational programs, second opinions, and doctor suggestions from around the nation.
