Liver Cancer Research Fund


The Liver Cancer Research Fund has spent the past few years maintaining the fight against Liver Cancer. They work with a best in class team of experts to push the currently established limits of medical knowledge through innovative clinical research.

The fund has seen a massive boost in demand over the past few years, something that they have handled with ease.

In 2010 alone they assisted over 3,000 patients through their Liver Tumor Clinic, and treated 400 patients with a Liver Cancer related operation. They are pioneering new surgery techniques that provide patients with less painful procedures and shorter recovery times.
