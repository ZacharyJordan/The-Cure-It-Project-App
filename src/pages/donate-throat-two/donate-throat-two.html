Oracle Cancer Trust


The Oracle Cancer Trust is a UK based charity organization that conducts research to ensure breakthroughs in head and neck cancer treatment.

Improving the life of patients and ending throat cancers by funding and performing experimental studies, quality of life analysis and clinical studies of ground-breaking anti-cancer therapies is their goal and numerous grants have been given to fund this.

The organization has been able to help fund three PhD projects recently and plans to expand to be able to conduct more research to fund specific projects like quality of voice, speech and swallowing function following laryngectomy.
