Advancements in Chemotherapy

Chemotherapy is the application of a powerful drug or combinations of drugs to combat cancerous cells in the body. Although it is a treatment option that has historically been associated with negative side effects and inconsistent results, recent developments have sought to upset that understanding through the careful refinement of the practice.

There exist many new chemotherapy drugs that are currently being studied in clinical trials. One such drug, Bendamustine, has proven to be effective against particularly aggressive cases of Lymphoma.

Although negative side effects were observed in several patients, developments such as this stand as a towering example of scientific advancements giving hope to people who otherwise have none.

It is believed that additional research can further refine these emerging technologies through the means of making them a more effective and safe option for patients around the world.
