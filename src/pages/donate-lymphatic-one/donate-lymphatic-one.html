Lymphoma Research Foundation

The Lymphoma Research Foundation is an organization that has long since established itself as one of the most reputable authorities in regards to the research and development of new Lymphatic Cancer treatments.

They support the fight against Lymphatic Cancer on fronts both scientific and informational, and in doing so are able to actively engage the public in combating the disease while also taking valuable strides towards improving the safety and comfort of patients around the world.

The organization is notable due to its tireless support of research efforts that ultimately elevate our knowledge of the causes of Lymphatic Cancers and how to effectively combat them.

The Lymphoma Research Foundation has awarded over $50 million to such efforts since its founding in 2011, and in doing so has taken meaninful strides towards making the issue of Lymphatic Cancers one of the past.

All of this has been achieved while maintaining a great deal of financial transparency. In accordance with this fact, Charity Navigator awarded them with a score of 97 out of 100 in regards to their accountability and transparency in regards to their finances and general operations.
