About this app Who are you? Topbox Software is a small team currently composed of Zachary Jordan and Ishan Taparia. We have regularly produced apps, websites, games, and everything in between since our formation in the summer of 2014. Why did you make this app? We created The Cure It Project after having difficulty finding simple, clear information about a certain type of cancer. While the current body of cancer information is broad, it can often be difficult to approach without some background in medicine. We hope our application fixes this problem. How can I reach you? If you have a story regarding cancer, be it you who was diagnosed or somebody that you know, we would love to hear from you. We can be reached at TopboxSoftware@gmail.com.
